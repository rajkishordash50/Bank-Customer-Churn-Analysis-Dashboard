# Bank-Customer-Churn-Analysis-Dashboard


## 🔹 **1. Project Title & Headline **

**Bank Customer Churn Analysis & Dashboard**
Bank Customer Churn Analysis
Analyzed customer data to understand why customers leave the bank.
Created a simple dashboard to identify patterns and key factors affecting churn.
Focused on improving customer retention through data insights.

---

## 🔹 **2. Short Description / Purpose**

This project analyzes bank customer data to understand why customers leave (churn). It aims to identify key risk factors and provide insights that help banks improve customer retention and engagement.

---

## 🔹 **3. Tech Stack**

* MS Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Dashboard)

---

## 🔹 **4. Data Source**

* Bank Customer Churn Dataset (structured dataset containing customer demographics, account details, and activity status)

---

## 🔹 **5. Features & Highlights**

### 🔸 **5.1 Business Problem**

Banks face significant revenue loss due to customer churn. Identifying at-risk customers and understanding churn behavior is critical for improving retention.

---

### 🔸 **5.2 Goal of the Dashboard**

* Detect customers likely to churn
* Analyze factors influencing churn (age, balance, activity, etc.)
* Provide a clear visual summary for decision-making

---

### 🔸 **5.3 Walkthrough of Key Visuals**

* **Churn Distribution Chart** → Shows proportion of customers who stayed vs left
* **Age vs Churn Analysis** → Highlights which age groups churn more
* **Balance vs Churn** → Identifies relationship between account balance and churn
* **Active vs Inactive Customers** → Compares churn rates based on activity status
* **Geography/Gender Analysis** → Shows demographic impact on churn

---

### 🔸 **5.4 Key Insights**

* Higher churn observed among **inactive customers**
* Customers with **higher balances** show increased churn risk
* Certain **age groups (mid-age customers)** are more likely to leave
* Engagement level is a major driver of retention

---

### 🔸 **5.5 Business Impact**

* Helps banks **identify high-risk customers early**
* Supports **targeted retention strategies** (offers, engagement campaigns)
* Improves **customer satisfaction and lifetime value**
* Enables **data-driven decision-making** for business growth
